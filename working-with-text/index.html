Unordered and Ordered lists are great for organizing information!

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists look like the following:

  • Thing 1
  • Thing 2
  • Thing 3

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists look like the following:

  1. Thing 1 is more important than thing 2
  2. Thing 2 is more important than thing 3
  3. Thing 3
